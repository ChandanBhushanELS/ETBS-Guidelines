ES Question

Question:

New computer viruses and other types of malware are released all the time. In addition to malware targeted toward computers, there is also mobile malware targeted to smartphones, tablets, and other internet-enabled devices. Many security companies, such as Symantec and McAfee, list the most recent security threats on their Web sites.

  1. Visit the Web site of Security Company, and then identify a current virus or worm.
  2. When was that virus or worm introduced? What does it do? How is it spread? To what type’s devices is it targeted?
  3. How many devices have been affected so far?
  4. Is there an estimated cost associated with that virus or worm?
  5. Is that virus or worm still in existence?
  6. Have security programs updated to locate and remove this malware? If not, is there a procedure for removing this virus or worm from an infected device?
  7. Prepare a one-page summary of your findings and submit it to your instructor.
