Purpose

Evelyn Learning strives to support all students in graduating, confident and prepared. One service that Evelyn Learning provides to help students get unstuck is access to high-quality solutions and explanations to textbook questions. Students may use these solutions to improve their understanding of a concept, refine their approach to solving a problem, or to confirm that their work is correct.

Evelyn Learning textbook solutions do not simply provide an answer to a question. Rather, they foster deeper learning by providing rich explanations, quality graphics, how-to videos, and step-by-step solutions to supplement students' learning. The treatment levels for different solutions can vary, but every solution should include a quality explanation to support meaningful learning.
